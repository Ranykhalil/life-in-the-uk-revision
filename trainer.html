How this works (the psychology)

Testing effect — you learn by retrieving, not re-reading. Every fact is a question.

Immediate correction — get it wrong and you see the right answer, then the question quietly returns 8–12 cards later (no warning — that’s deliberate), and once more ~15–20 cards after that. Only two spaced correct answers clear it.

Spacing — each question lives in a zone (1→5). Right answers move it up; the app waits longer each time before re-asking (15 min → 2 h → 12 h → 2 days). A wrong answer sends it back to Zone 1.

Known = proven, then retired — a question only counts as known (Zone 4+) after several separate, spaced correct answers. Once proven it stops appearing, so training time goes to the questions you’ve been getting wrong.

Interleaving & shuffling — topics mix together and answer options shuffle every time, so you learn the facts, not the button positions.
